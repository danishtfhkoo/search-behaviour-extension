# Privacy Policy — Search Logger Chrome Extension

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**Extension name:** Search Logger  
**Developer:** Danish Khoo / Utrecht University
**Contact:** m.d.khoo@students.uu.nl

---

## 1. Who this policy applies to

This extension is made available exclusively to individuals who have been formally recruited as participants in an approved academic research study. It is not a general-purpose product. If you have not been recruited into the study, you should not install this extension.

---

## 2. What data is collected

When a research session is active, the extension records the following events from your Google Images searches:

| Data type            | What is recorded                                                                              |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Search queries       | The text you type into the Google Images search box                                           |
| Query reformulations | How your query changed relative to the previous one (e.g. words added, removed, or rewritten) |
| Image clicks         | Whether you clicked on an image result (including thumbnail URL, source page URL, and domain) |
| Image saves          | Images you explicitly mark as saved using the in-extension save button or keyboard shortcut   |
| Filter interactions  | Which Google Images filters you applied (e.g. size, colour, type)                             |
| Scroll depth         | How far down the results page you scrolled, recorded in 10% increments                        |
| Dwell time           | How long an image preview panel remained open                                                 |

No data is collected outside of an active session. Sessions are started and ended manually by the researcher or participant using the extension popup.

---

## 3. What data is NOT collected

- Passwords, form inputs, or any content outside of Google Images search pages
- Browsing history from other websites or tabs
- Personal communications
- Any data from pages other than `www.google.com` and regional Google domains (e.g. `www.google.co.uk`)
- Any data when a session is not active

---

## 4. How data is stored

All data is stored **locally on your device** using the Chrome browser's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`). No data is transmitted to any remote server, cloud service, or third party at any point.

At the end of each session, a single JSON file is exported to your device's download folder. This file is the complete record of that session. The extension retains no copy after export.

---

## 5. How data is used

The exported session files are used solely for the purposes of the academic research study for which you were recruited. The data will be analysed to understand how people search for images under different task conditions. All analysis is conducted in accordance with the study's ethics approval.

Data will be anonymised or pseudonymised before any publication or presentation of findings.

---

## 6. Data sharing

Session data is not shared with Google, Anthropic, browser vendors, advertisers, or any other third party. Data is shared only among the research team named in your participant information sheet.

---

## 7. Your rights

As a research participant, your rights regarding your data are set out in the participant information sheet and consent form provided to you by the research team. You may withdraw from the study and request deletion of your session data by contacting the researcher directly.

---

## 8. Host permissions

The extension requests access to regional Google domains (e.g. `google.com`, `google.co.uk`, `google.nl`) because participants may be located in different countries and access Google through their local domain. The extension only activates on Google Images result pages; it does not read or interact with any other content on these domains.

---
